The Xbox Series X and Playstation 5:

How the new generation of consoles defines gaming.

Written by THEJAVADRINKER

With the introduction of the Xbox Series X on the 10th of November, 2020 and the Playstation 5 on the 12th of November, 2020, we have entered the 9th generation of video game consoles. Now that the Xbox One and the Playstation 4 have been virtually left behind by Microsoft and Sony, we can now experience all of the new benefits of these new consoles.
