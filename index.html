The Xbox Series X and Playstation 5:

How the new generation of consoles defines gaming.

Written by THEJAVADRINKER

With the introduction of the Xbox Series X on the 10th of November, 2020 and the Playstation 5 on the 12th of November, 2020, we have entered the 9th generation of video game consoles. Now that the Xbox One and the Playstation 4 have been virtually left behind by Microsoft and Sony, we can now experience all of the new benefits of these new consoles. But before you go grabbing your wallet to go purchase the new console, you may be thinking: "What eaxctly does the Xbox Series X/PS5 have that makes it better than the Xbox One/PS4?". This article will aim to answer that exact question, by detailing all of the new features and telling you if it is worth it.

TRUE 4K GAMING

With improved graphics compared to the last generation of consoles, you will be able to enjoy your games with the highest quality, as the developers intended for you to see it. These graphics are even able to compete with many high-end PCs and gaming laptops.

UP TO 120FPS

Unlike the last generation of consoles, whoch could only typically run games at a slow frame-rate of 30FPS, you will be able to enjoy your games at fast frame-rates of 120FPS, allowing you to experience the game as if you were inside the world of it.
